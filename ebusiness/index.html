About Kinasoru East Africa

Our Story

Kinasoru East Africa (T) Ltd is a privately owned agricultural company, established and legally registered in February 2016. Founded by Amani Ng'oma, an Agro-Entrepreneur and Agricultural Economist from Sokoine University of Agriculture, the company is deeply rooted in the agricultural sector, focusing on both crop and livestock value chains.

In the crop value chain, Kinasoru specializes in the production and supply of a wide range of fruits, vegetables, roots, and tubers. On the livestock side, the company focuses on poultry production and supply, including day-old chicks, fertilized eggs, and poultry products.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of agricultural products do you offer?

Kinasoru East Africa offers a wide range of fresh and high-quality agricultural products, including fruits, vegetables, roots and tubers, spices, cereals and legumes, livestock breeds, and animal feeds. We are committed to providing reliable and sustainable supply chains for our valued customers.

What kind of agribusiness consultancy services do you provide?

Our agribusiness consultancy unit provides comprehensive services to help organizations effectively engage in the agricultural sector in Tanzania. We offer Farm Audits, Business Modelling, Technical and Entrepreneurship Skills Trainings, Business Plan Development, Project Feasibility Studies, Farm Tours and Workshops, Farm Supervision, Agricultural Input Supply, Agricultural Extension Service, Agricultural Marketing Research and Market Linkage, and more.

How does Kinasoru contribute to community development?

Kinasoru is dedicated to positively impacting communities in Tanzania. We contribute to poverty reduction through employment creation, particularly for youth and women. We also provide a platform for local producers and smallholder farmers to benefit through knowledge transfer, contract farming, and market assurance. Additionally, we are committed to addressing nutritional and food security challenges in the society.

What are Kinasoru's core values?

Our core values guide our actions and shape our company culture: Accountability, Integrity, Transparency, Social Responsibility, Service and Operational Excellence.

Vision

To partner with governmental and commercial entities in realizing strategic agriculture development and achieving long-term growth and sustainability in the East African region.

Mission

To grow into a sustainable agricultural and food products provider and partner, ensuring supply sufficiency, high quality of farming and production operations, and protection of the environment while empowering smallholders.

Impact

Kinasoru East Africa (T) Ltd operations bring about several benefits to the society and the country's economy: Addressing nutritional and food security challenges, Contributing to poverty reduction through employment creation, Providing a platform for local producers and smallholder farmers to benefit, and Generating foreign currency through exportation.
